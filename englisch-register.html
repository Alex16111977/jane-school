Greetings & Farewells

Good morning vs. Hey — как поздороваться в любой ситуации

👋 Приветствие — Greetings

formal 👔 neutral 👥 informal 🗣
Good morning, Mr/Ms … Good morning! Morning! / Hey!
Good afternoon / Good evening Hello! / Hi! Hey! / Yo! / Sup?
How do you do? How are you? What's up? / How's it going? / Howdy?
It's a pleasure to meet you. Nice to meet you! Hey, nice to meet ya!
Welcome! We're glad you could join us. Welcome! Good to see you. Finally you're here! / Oh hey!
I trust you are well. Hope you're doing well! Hope you're good! / You good?

👏 Прощание — Farewells

formal 👔 neutral 👥 informal 🗣
Goodbye. Bye! / See you! Later! / Peace! / Catch ya!
Good night. Good night! Night! / Sleep tight!
It was a pleasure speaking with you. Great talking to you! That was fun! / Was nice chatting!
I look forward to our next meeting. See you next time! See ya! / Until next time!
I wish you a pleasant day. Have a great day! Have a good one! / Take care!
Yours sincerely / Kind regards Best wishes / Take care Cheers / xoxo / ttyl

Requests, Thanks & Apologies

Просьбы, благодарность, извинения — в трёх стилях

🙏 Просьба — Requests

formal neutral informal
I would be grateful if you could … Could you please …? Hey, can you …?
I would like to request … Can you help me with …? Do me a favour and …
Would you be so kind as to …? Could you possibly …? Mind doing …? / Can you just …?
I wish to draw your attention to … I just wanted to mention that … Hey, quick heads up: …
I am writing to enquire about … I wanted to ask about … Just wondering about …

🙌 Благодарность — Thanks

formal neutral informal
I am sincerely grateful for … Thank you very much! Thanks! / Cheers! / Ta!
I would like to express my gratitude. Thanks a lot! / Many thanks! You're a legend! / Thanks a bunch!
I am deeply indebted to you for … That was really kind of you. That was so nice of you!
Not at all. / My pleasure. You're welcome! / No problem! No worries! / Anytime! / All good!

😕 Извинение — Apologies

formal neutral informal
I sincerely apologise for … I'm sorry! / I apologise. Sorry! / My bad! / Oops!
I would like to apologise for … I'm sorry that … Sorry, didn't mean that!
This was an oversight on my part. That was my mistake. My fault! / That's on me!
I can assure you this will not happen again. I won't let that happen again. Won't happen again, promise!
Please accept my apologies. / Think nothing of it. No problem! / That's okay! It's fine! / Don't sweat it!

Conversation & Reactions

Как реагировать, поддерживать разговор, выражать мнение

💬 Реакции — Reactions

Смысл formal informal
согласие I concur. / I agree entirely. Exactly! / Totally! / 100%! / Fr!
несогласие I beg to differ. / I disagree. No way! / Nah! / That's not it!
удивление I find that rather surprising. No way! / Seriously?! / That's wild!

неодобрение I have reservations about that. That's a mess. / Not it. / Cringe!

повторите Could you please repeat that? Huh? / What? / Say that again?

formal informal

It would appear that … It looks like … / Seems like …
I consider it important that … I think it's really important that …
It is worth noting that … Also, don't forget that …
To summarise, it can be said that … So basically, … / Long story short: …

Emails & Letters

Dear Sir/Madam vs. Hey — структура письма в любом стиле

💬 Обращение — Opening

formal neutral / semi-formal informal
Dear Sir/Madam, Hello, Hey [Name]! / Hi there,
Dear Mr/Ms [Surname], Hello Mr/Ms [Name], Hi [Name], / Dear [Name],
To Whom It May Concern, Hi everyone, Hey you! / Hiya!

📝 Начало письма — Opening lines

formal informal
I am writing to enquire about … Just wanted to drop you a message about …
With reference to your email of … I would like to … Got your message — thanks!
I am writing to apply for the position of … Hey, I saw your ad and I'm interested!
I would like to bring to your attention that … Just a heads up — …
I am pleased to inform you that … Great news: …

✅ Завершение письма — Closing lines

formal informal
I look forward to hearing from you. Write back soon!
Please do not hesitate to contact me. Hit me up if you need anything!
Yours sincerely, [Full Name] Best, / Cheers, / [Name]
Yours faithfully, [Full Name] Talk soon! / Bye for now!
Kind regards / Best regards xoxo / ttyl / Take care!
